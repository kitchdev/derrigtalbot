Team

Derrig & Talbot was created through the merger of Derrig Construction and Talbot Properties. Derrig Construction was a general contractor founded by Ryan Derrig specializing in the residential and commercial markets. Talbot Properties was a property developer founded by Marc Talbot. After years of working together on various projects they decided, in 2014, to join forces in order to create a full service Design-Build firm. The team is comprised of experts in various fields. The company employs project managers and project administrators, carpenters, electricians, plumbers etc. Derrig & Talbot has tackled projects in a wide range of sectors: from sleek modern condos to the construction of dental clinics. The company’s experience allows us to tackle almost any project.
